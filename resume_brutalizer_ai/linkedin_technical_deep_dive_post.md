## SETUP / CONTEXT
After analyzing 215 Indian AI Engineering Job Descriptions (2026 data), I realized most developers are flying blind. Their resumes don't match what companies are *actually* hiring for. So, I decided to build a solution to bridge that gap.

## DATA / BRUTAL REALITY
My analysis revealed a stark reality:
- **87%** of AI roles demand RAG/LangChain skills.
- **76%** require Vertex AI or Gemini experience.
- **64%** look for FastAPI expertise.

Yet, most resumes I've seen barely touch on these critical skills. This isn't just a skill gap; it's a **visibility gap**.

## SOLUTION / WHAT I BUILT
I developed **"Resume Brutalizer"** – a full-stack AI tool designed to give developers an unfair advantage. It doesn't just score your resume; it provides an actionable roadmap to get hired in 2026.

**Key Features:**
1.  **Resume Audit:** Upload your PDF resume, and it's analyzed against real 2026 Indian AI JDs, giving you an overall score (0-100) and a "Have vs. Have-Not" skill breakdown.
2.  **Skill Learning Roadmap:** Get a personalized 30-day learning plan for your missing skills, complete with resources and difficulty ratings.
3.  **Resume Rewrite Suggestions:** AI-powered suggestions to rephrase your resume sections, highlighting keywords and impact.
4.  **Skill Mastery Timeline:** Understand the estimated days to master specific skills, from your current level to an advanced or expert stage, including prerequisites.

## HOW IT WORKS / TECH DEEP DIVE
Building this required a robust, modern stack:
-   **Frontend:** React 19 + Tailwind CSS for a responsive, dark-themed UI.
-   **Backend:** Express.js with tRPC for type-safe API communication.
-   **Database:** MySQL to store audit history and user data.
-   **AI Engine:** Google Gemini API for advanced resume analysis, skill extraction, roadmap generation, rewrite suggestions, and mastery timeline predictions.
-   **Authentication:** Manus OAuth for secure user sessions.
-   **Deployment:** Hosted on Cloud Run for serverless scalability.

Every decision, from the database schema to the UI components, was made to deliver a fast, accurate, and brutal user experience. The integration of Gemini AI was crucial for generating the detailed, personalized feedback that goes beyond generic advice.
